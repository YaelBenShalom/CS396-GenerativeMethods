Assignment 8 - Big Data

In this assignment, I created an interactive app to browse and visualize data

👍🏼 🦾 😂 My Emoji Distribution Visualization App 😂 🦾 👍🏼

Hover on the graph to discover the distribution of your favorite emoji

This graph presents the usage distribution of 845 emoji characters, computed from all tweets until June 4th, 2015.
The bars are organized by the number of occurrences, from left to right.

Hovering over a bar will present the emoji image, the number of occurrences, and the occurrence percent out of all the emoji occurrences.
